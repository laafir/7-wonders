Christ the Redeemer (Portuguese: Cristo Redentor, standard Brazilian Portuguese: [ËˆkÉ¾istu Êedáº½ËˆtoÊ]) is an Art Deco statue of Jesus in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, created by French-Polish sculptor Paul Landowski and built by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa, in collaboration with French engineer Albert Caquot.
